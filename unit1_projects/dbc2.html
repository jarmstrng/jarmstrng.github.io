Best Oatmeal Cookie in the World

Extra tips gleaned from the NYT article on chocolate chip cookies:

  • Chill dough 36 hrs
  • Make cookies 6" big
  • Use 60% or more cacao chips, or couverture chocolate
  • 2:3 chocolate:dough ratio
  • Use room temp ingredients
  • Don't overmix

South Street Inn Oatmeal Chocolate Chip Cookie Recipe

  • 2 3⁄4 cups all purpose flour
  • 1 tsp Baking soda
  • 1⁄2 tsp salt
  • 1 1⁄4 cups packed brown sugar
  • 1 cups butter softened
  • 1⁄2 cup granulated sugar
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 tbsp. milk
  • 2 tsp vanilla
  • 2 1⁄2 cups old fashioned oats
  • 2 cups chocolate chips
  • 1 cup chopped walnuts (optional)

  1. Combine flour, baking soda and salt in a small bowl.
  2. Beat brown sugar, butter, granulated sugar in large bowl until creamy.
  3. Add eggs milk and vanilla.
  4. Gradually beat in flour mixture.
  5. Add in oats nuts and chocolate: mix well.
  6. Chill dough in fridge at least an hour (or 12-36 hours) before baking to promote cookies ‘setting up.’
  7. Drop by rounded tablespoon onto a cookie sheet 9 per sheet as the cookies spread out quite a bit.
  8. Bake at 325 F for about 10 to 14 minutes (try 350F for non- convection ovens).
